About

At the very beginning the band was presented as a one-man band from Krasnodar region. In the summer of 2020 a vocalist from another corner of our vastness joined the band and in this connection it is easier to say that the band is from Russia. The music in the artist's understanding is a mixture of melancholic melodies combined with the sound of 90s metal bands.

  • Based in: Russia
  • Genre: Doom
  • Founded in May 5, 2015
  • Paper sails
